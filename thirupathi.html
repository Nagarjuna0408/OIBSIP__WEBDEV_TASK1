About Tirupati Temple

The Tirupati Balaji Temple, located in the hill town of Tirumala in Andhra Pradesh, is one of the most visited and richest religious shrines in the world. Dedicated to Lord Venkateswara, the temple is an important pilgrimage site attracting millions of devotees every year.
